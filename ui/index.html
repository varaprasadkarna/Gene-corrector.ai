About GeneCorrect AI

Our Mission

GeneCorrect AI is a state-of-the-art platform designed to empower biologists and genetic researchers. Our mission is to provide a highly accurate, fast, and intuitive tool for identifying DNA mutations in key genes like CFTR and DSCAM, and to leverage generative AI to propose corrected, non-mutated sequences.

How to Use the Platform

  1. Login: Access the platform using your biologist credentials. If you don't have an account, you can create one from the login page.
  2. Enter Sequence: Paste a raw DNA sequence (containing only A, C, G, T) into the text area on the main dashboard.
  3. Analyze: Click the "Analyze Sequence" button to submit the data to our pipeline.
  4. View Results: The system will quickly classify the gene type, determine its mutation status, and if a mutation is found, it will generate a corrected, healthy version of the sequence.

The Technology

Our pipeline integrates multiple machine learning models. A high-performance Logistic Regression model first classifies the gene and its mutation status. For mutated sequences, a sophisticated sequence-to-sequence (Seq2Seq) neural network, trained on vast genetic datasets, generates a corrected sequence.
